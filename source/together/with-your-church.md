# With Your Church

- Acts 5:29-39
- Romans 3:23
- John 14:26
- 1 Corinthians 12:4-11
- Ephesians 4:11-16
    - What is the purpose behind everyone having different gifts and roles within the church? What is the end goal?
    - In what ways have your church leaders helped you become a more mature Christian?
    - What could it look like for you to prioritize praying over the leaders in your church?
    - How could you begin asking God to equip those who are trying to equip others?
    - Are you required to believe that the entirety of the bible is true to have salvation?
    - How has growing in your faith made you more stable?
    - In what ways are you able to discern what is right and wrong?
    - What would it look like to commit your resources and talents to God's people?
- Hebrews 10:19–25
    - In what ways have other Christians encouraged you to be more like Jesus? In what ways are you doing the same for others?
    - What are some tangible ways that you could hold yourself, and your friends, accountable for staying plugged into your local church?
- Colossians 3:16
